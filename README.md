## 🧠 The Blank Collar

<table>
<tr>
<td width="60%">

The **Blank Collar** is not a company. It is a philosophy.

White collar. Blue collar. Both defined by what humans do for work. The blank collar asks: **what happens when work no longer defines us?**

For most of human history, we built tools to help us work harder. We are now, for the first time, building tools that simply do the work. The question that follows is not economic. It is existential.

My answer: *this is the greatest opportunity in human history.*

Not a threat. An invitation. To build businesses differently. To lead organisations without the old overhead. To spend human attention on things only humans can do.

The Blank Collar is where this philosophy becomes practice. I work with founders and executives at the precise moment AI can change how their organisation operates, what it costs, and what it becomes capable of.

## 🔐 What I Am Building Now

### Cybee.ai — Agentic Security for the World's SMEs

<div align="center">

| | |
|---|---|
| **Stage** | Pre-revenue · Seed round in progress |
| **Raise** | €750K pre-seed raised · €1.5M seed at €10M post |
| **Market** | 400M+ SMEs globally with no real security |
| **Model** | Channel-first · Partner-led distribution |
| **Edge** | Agentic security layer that acts, not just alerts |

</div>

<br/>

> The world's 400 million small businesses are the largest unprotected attack surface on the internet. Enterprise security tools were not built for them. They are too complex, too expensive, and require expertise these teams do not have.
>
> Cybee.ai changes this by deploying an agentic security layer that does not just monitor threats — it responds to them. Automatically. Continuously. Without requiring a CISO on staff.

**What makes Cybee different:**

- 🤖 **Agentic by design** — security that acts, not just reports
- 🌍 **SME-native** — built for teams with no dedicated IT, not scaled down from enterprise
- 🔗 **Channel-first** — distributed through accounting firms, MSPs, and compliance partners including BDO Switzerland
- 📋 **Compliance-ready** — NIS2, DORA, GDPR coverage built in
- 🧠 **OpenClaw OSB** — proprietary 18-control hardening framework for agentic AI systems

**Active partnerships:** BDO Switzerland · OSF Digital · Scytale.ai · Harochi · Yum! Brands (POC across 4 regions) · JYSK
