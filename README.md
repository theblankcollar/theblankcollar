## 🚀 What I Am Building

### The Blank Collar — AI Agentic Transformation Agency

<div align="center">

| | |
|---|---|
| **What we do** | We transform how businesses operate using AI agents |
| **Who we work with** | Global enterprises · Established SMBs · Ambitious startups |
| **How we work** | We do not consult and leave. We build and embed. |
| **The result** | Organisations that run leaner, move faster, and free their people to do meaningful work |

</div>

<br/>

> Most companies are putting AI on top of broken processes and calling it transformation.
>
> We go deeper. We rebuild the operating layer itself — automating workflows end to end, deploying agents that handle entire functions, and creating infrastructure that learns and improves continuously.
>
> The outcome is not a more efficient version of what existed before. It is something structurally different.

**What agentic transformation actually means in practice:**

- 🤖 **Agents that run processes** — not copilots that help humans run processes
- 🔄 **End to end automation** of operations, communications, compliance, and intelligence
- 🧠 **Institutional memory** that does not leave when people do
- 📊 **Decision infrastructure** that makes organisations faster without making them fragile
- 🌍 **Built for scale** — from a 5-person startup to a 5,000-person enterprise

### 🌐 blankcollar.ai — The Company Memory Layer

<div align="center">

[![blankcollar.ai](https://img.shields.io/badge/Visit-blankcollar.ai-6C63FF?style=for-the-badge&logo=globe&logoColor=white)](https://blankcollar.ai)

</div>

<br/>

One of the most expensive problems in any organisation is invisible: **knowledge that lives only in people's heads.**

When someone leaves, that knowledge leaves with them. When a team scales, context breaks down. When decisions get made, institutional memory is not in the room.

**blankcollar.ai is the answer.**

It is the memory layer we built for organisations — a system that captures, organises, and surfaces the knowledge that actually runs a business. Not documents. Not wikis. Living, queryable, agentic organisational intelligence.

Every company we transform gets this as the foundation. Because transformation without memory is just expensive change management.

## 📂 What Came Before

<details>
<summary><strong>🏦 Numarics — Built From Zero, Backed by UBS, Sold</strong></summary>
<br/>

Numarics was a Swiss AI-powered financial management platform built for the businesses that traditional finance tools always ignored or overwhelmed: SMEs, founders, and the self-employed.

We combined bookkeeping, tax preparation, payroll, and financial intelligence into a single AI-native platform that a founder could actually use without a finance team sitting beside them. The product read invoices, categorised transactions automatically, prepared tax filings, and surfaced financial insights in real time — turning what was previously a quarterly headache into a continuous, invisible background process.

I co-founded the company and served as CXO, owning product vision, brand, marketing, and nearshore operations. We secured backing from UBS — one of the most demanding and credibility-defining investors in the Swiss ecosystem — and scaled a team across Switzerland and the Balkans.

The company was successfully merged with Radicant, completing a clean exit.

The lesson that stayed with me: the businesses that need the most sophisticated tools are always the last ones the industry designs for. Every company I have built since has started from that insight.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>📡 Dentsu — Senior Global Leadership (via gyro acquisition, 2015)</strong></summary>
<br/>

Joined Dentsu through the acquisition of gyro in 2015 and operated at senior level inside one of the world's largest marketing and communications networks. Led global transformation initiatives including Project Redwood and a large-scale enterprise data programme called Samurai.

Two decades of working inside organisations of this size taught me something uncomfortable: the larger the company, the more energy goes into maintaining the structure rather than creating value. AI changes that equation entirely. That tension is what eventually pushed me to build rather than operate inside someone else's machine.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>📺 Mandeta — Programmatic Infrastructure for Albanian-Speaking Markets</strong></summary>
<br/>

Co-founded Mandeta alongside Managing Director Donjeta Jakupi. Building the first purpose-built programmatic advertising infrastructure for Albanian-speaking audiences — one of the largest linguistically cohesive diaspora communities in Europe, almost entirely invisible to mainstream ad technology.

The Albanian diaspora across Germany, Switzerland, Austria, and the broader DACH region represents a high-value, underserved audience that major media groups have consistently failed to reach. Mandeta changes that.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🎯 Advisory and Strategic Work</strong></summary>
<br/>

I work with a small number of founders and leadership teams at critical inflection points — preparing for fundraising, rethinking operating models ahead of scale, or navigating the transition to AI-native operations. I bring pattern recognition from two decades across global enterprises, high-growth startups, and successful exits. I do not take many of these engagements. The ones I take, I take seriously.
